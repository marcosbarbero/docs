# Privacy Policy for KineticCut

**Effective Date:** 2026-06-05

KineticCut is a client-side iOS application built by Marcos Barbero, an independent developer. KineticCut is designed around a single principle: your footage and everything derived from it stays on your device.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

- **No Remote Data Collection.** KineticCut does not collect, harvest, or transmit any personal data, analytics, behavioural tracking, or device identifiers. There is no server, no account, and no third-party SDK.
- **Local Processing Environment.** All application logic — importing videos, analysing motion and aesthetics with Apple's Vision framework, building the clip ledger, and trimming exports — happens locally within the app's sandbox on your iOS device. KineticCut makes no network calls of its own.
- **Photos / Media.** KineticCut reads only the specific videos you select through Apple's system photo picker. It does not request access to your full photo library, and it never uploads or copies your library; selected videos are read locally and released when your session ends. Exported clips are handed to the standard iOS share sheet, which you control.

## 2. In-App Subscriptions

The Pro Creator Pass is an auto-renewable subscription managed securely through the Apple App Store framework (StoreKit). KineticCut does not process or retain payment details, financial records, or personal identity information — Apple handles all of that. KineticCut cannot see your Apple Account, your payment method, or your billing history.

## 3. Children's Privacy

KineticCut does not collect any data from anyone, including children, and contains no advertising or tracking.
