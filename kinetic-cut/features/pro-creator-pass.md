# Pro Creator Pass

> Go unlimited: bigger batches, smarter sorting, and batch export — all still 100% on device.

## What it does

The Pro Creator Pass unlocks KineticCut's full triage suite. You can import as many videos per session as you like, use advanced motion-sorting presets, pull expression timestamps automatically, export several slices at once, and choose custom slice lengths. It's a subscription you can pay monthly or yearly.

## When to use it

- You routinely work with more than three clips at a time.
- You want to isolate a specific kind of shot (e.g. only whip-pans) quickly.
- You export several slices per session and want them all in one go.

## How it works

1. Hit a Pro feature — the import cap, multi-clip export, or tap **Go Pro** on the Export tab.
2. The paywall shows the monthly and yearly options.
3. Choose a plan and confirm with your Apple Account; the unlock is immediate.
4. Already subscribed on another device? Tap **Restore Purchases**.

## Free vs Premium

- **Free:** 3 videos per session, single-clip export, standard 3-second slices.
- **Pro Creator Pass (€2.99/month or €24.99/year):** unlimited batch imports, advanced motion-sorting presets, automated expression-timestamp extraction, multi-clip batch export, and custom 1s / 3s / 5s slice lengths.

## Privacy

Everything stays on device. Purchases are handled by Apple's App Store — KineticCut never sees your payment details, and unlocking Pro adds no tracking, analytics, or network activity to the app itself.

## Known limits

- Subscriptions renew through your Apple Account and are managed in iOS Settings.
- If no active subscription is found, **Restore Purchases** will tell you so.
- Pricing shown in the app reflects your local App Store storefront.
