# KineticCut

KineticCut turns hours of raw footage into short-form gold — on your device. Hand it a small batch of videos and it finds the moments worth using: smooth camera moves, sharp shots, the second someone looks at the lens. Everything runs locally with Apple's Vision engine. No servers, no uploads, no accounts.

## Features

- [**Scoped Media Intake**]({{ site.baseurl }}/kinetic-cut/features/scoped-media-intake/) — hand it just the videos you choose, nothing else from your library.
- [**Kinetic Motion & Aesthetic Analysis**]({{ site.baseurl }}/kinetic-cut/features/kinetic-analysis/) — on-device motion, aesthetic, and gaze detection.
- [**3-Second Triage & Export**]({{ site.baseurl }}/kinetic-cut/features/triage-and-export/) — pick the slices you want and trim them to your timeline, non-destructively.
- [**Pro Creator Pass**]({{ site.baseurl }}/kinetic-cut/features/pro-creator-pass/) — unlimited batches, advanced presets, and batch export.

## Why KineticCut

- **100% on device.** Video analysis uses Apple's Vision framework and the Neural Engine — frames never leave your phone.
- **No servers, no analytics, no third-party SDKs.** Nothing is collected, tracked, or transmitted.
- **Non-destructive.** Your clips are tracked as lightweight time-pointers; the original footage is never copied or altered.
- **Works offline.** The only network it ever touches is Apple's App Store, to manage your subscription.
