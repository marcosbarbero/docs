# Scribe Pass

> One subscription that unlocks unlimited recording, AI insights, translation, and export — with no account and nothing leaving your phone.

## What it does

Scribe Pass is VoiceScribe's optional subscription. The app is fully usable for free; Scribe Pass removes the limits and unlocks the AI features. There's no sign-up and no account — the subscription is tied to your Apple ID, so it works on every device you sign into and restores with one tap.

## When to use it

- You record meetings longer than an hour.
- You want AI summaries, key highlights, and action items.
- You need to translate transcripts or export them as Markdown / text.

## How it works

1. Tap **Get Scribe Pass** (on the Vault, or when you reach a premium feature).
2. Choose **Monthly (€1.00)** or **Yearly (€10.00)** and subscribe with your Apple ID.
3. Premium unlocks immediately, everywhere in the app.
4. On a new device, tap **Restore Purchases** to bring your subscription across — no account needed.

## Free vs Premium

- **Free:** recording up to **1 hour** per session, on-device transcription, automatic paragraph formatting, plain-text copy, browse/search the vault, and on-device entity tags.
- **Scribe Pass:** **unlimited** continuous recording, AI **Smart Summary / Key Highlights / Action Items**, **transcript translation**, and **Markdown / text export**.

## Privacy

Everything stays on device. VoiceScribe does not collect, transmit, or persist any data off your device. Purchases are handled by Apple’s App Store; your entitlement is verified **on-device** with no server of ours and no account — VoiceScribe never sees a payment detail.

## Known limits

- Prices shown come from the App Store in your local currency; €1.00 / €10.00 are the reference prices.
- Subscriptions renew automatically until cancelled, and are managed in your Apple ID settings.
- AI features still require a device that supports on-device intelligence; where it isn't available, those features say so even with Scribe Pass.
