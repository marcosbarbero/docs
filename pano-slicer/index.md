# PanoSlice

iOS panorama + profile-grid slicer. Slice one wide photo into an Instagram carousel or a 3-column profile mosaic, fully on-device.

## How it works

PanoSlice runs entirely on your iPhone. There are no servers, no analytics SDKs, no third-party services. Photos go from your library, through Apple's `ImageIO` and `CoreGraphics` frameworks, back into your camera roll.

The slicing modes:

- **Carousel** — slice a wide photo into 2–10 swipeable tiles that reassemble into one continuous image as your followers swipe.
- **Profile Grid** — turn one photo into a 3-column mosaic on your Instagram profile (3×1 Free, or 3×2 / 3×3 / 3×4 with Premium). PanoSlice writes the tiles in the right order so posting them in camera-roll order reassembles the image on your grid.

Premium ($-/mo or $-/yr — check the App Store for current pricing) unlocks every tile count and grid layout, removes the watermark, and preserves Display P3 wide colour end-to-end for HDR-capable devices.
